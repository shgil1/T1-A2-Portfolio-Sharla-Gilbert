ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Sharla, I am currently changing careers from Paramedicine to Web Development and I couldn't be more excited! I am passionate about accessible healthcare and working in this sector for the last 10 years has shown me that there is a huge need for tech in this area. Working directly in the community and helping others has given me the opportunity to experience the beauty of all walks of life and I'm ready to put that knowledge to a new sector. Follow along my journey, I would love to connect with you!
